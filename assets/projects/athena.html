Overview

As team lead for the Athena project at Georgia Tech's LIDAR Lab, I managed the development of a complex biomimetic upper body robot:

  • Led a team of 7 undergraduate students in system design and integration
  • Implemented a 28-DOF system using linear actuators to mimic human muscle architecture
  • Managed CAD repositories and performed topology optimization for weight reduction

The Athena project aims to advance our understanding of human-like movement and control in robotic systems, with potential applications in prosthetics and human-robot interaction.

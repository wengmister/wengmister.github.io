Overview

At Harmonic Bionics, I worked on the Harmony SHR, a 14-DoF upper-extremity rehabilitation robot designed to support natural arm movement. My role combined mechanical design, analysis, and prototype development.

I contributed to key mechatronic subsystems, designed custom linear-actuator assemblies and handheld devices, and built fixtures for sensor and mechanism testing. I also performed structural and fatigue analysis using FEA to validate component performance.

Beyond hardware, I helped establish the company's R&D infrastructure, launching the machine shop, writing safety procedures, and training engineers on prototyping equipment.

Overview

During my time at Harmonic Bionics, I contributed to the development of the Harmony SHR, an advanced upper extremity rehabilitation robot. My responsibilities included:

  • Design of major mechatronic systems using various manufacturing techniques
  • Development of handheld interface devices and custom linear actuator systems
  • Material validation through simulation and experimental testing
  • Integration of mechanical and electrical systems for prototype development

The Harmony SHR is designed to facilitate natural movement patterns during rehabilitation, allowing therapists to deliver high quality care while collecting objective patient data.

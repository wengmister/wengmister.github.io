Overview

Working in the EPIC Lab at Georgia Tech, I contributed to the development of an assistive hip exoskeleton designed to reduce the metabolic cost of walking:

  • Design and fabrication of series elastic actuator housings and components
  • Development of experimental protocols for system characterization
  • Collection and analysis of metabolic cost data under various conditions

This research aimed to develop more effective assistive devices for individuals with mobility impairments, with a focus on improving energy efficiency during walking.

Overview

The Monarch Platform is a major breakthrough in robotic-assisted surgery, especially for minimally invasive procedures involving the lungs and kidneys.

As a senior mechanical engineer currently working at J&J, I am mainly responsible for system development of the Monarch Surgical Platform, the first robotic platform for minimally invasive diagnostic and therapeutic procedures in the lungs and kidneys.

I work on a variety of projects focused on the robotic arm and instrument manipulator, addressing both hardware and software challenges. Additionally, I support the hardware team in advancing the overall platform development.

Overview

At Sunday Robotics, I work as the Robotics Systems Lead on Memo, a helpful home robot designed to take everyday chores — like dishes and laundry — off your plate.

I lead the team on developing Memo toward more reliable deployment, building out new software and hardware stacks along with the manufacturing tooling to produce them at scale.

My work also spans evaluating and integrating new components into the platform and validating robot performance across real-world deployment scenarios. Most recently, I worked on overall robot reliability for the ACT-2 preview, supporting the demo push with rapid robot modifications and hands-on engineering fixes.

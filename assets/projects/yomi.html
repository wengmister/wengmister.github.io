Overview

At Neocis, I was heavily involved in developing the second generation of the Yomi surgical system, the only FDA-cleared robotic device for dental implant surgery. My major contributions included:

  • Design and development of joint actuators for a 7-DOF robotic arm
  • Creation of compact robot end-effector for tool integration and user interaction
  • Implementation of kinematic analysis and simulation for joint load optimization
  • Development of calibration algorithms and inverse kinematics solver to enable obstacle avoidance capabilities

The Yomi system provides haptic guidance to dental surgeons while maintaining their direct control of the surgical instruments, combining the precision of robotic technology with the expertise of human practitioners.

Overview

At Neocis, I helped develop the Yomi S platform, the only FDA-cleared robotic system for dental implant surgery. My work centered on mechanical design, system integration, and calibration.

I engineered the platform's main actuated guidance arm, designed multiple generations of compact joint actuators for a 7-DoF robot, and built the prototypes used in testing. I also created a human-robot-interface end-effector providing haptic and visual feedback for surgical guidance.

To improve system accuracy, I developed Python-based kinematic simulators and a supervised-learning calibration method. I contributed across design reviews, documentation, and onboarding, helping move the next-generation system toward production.

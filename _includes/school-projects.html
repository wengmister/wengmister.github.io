Academic Projects

  • Grab-A-Pen Challenge - Northwestern MS Robotics

    Grab-A-Pen is the final challenge in the MSR program orientation hackathon.

    In this challenge, I opitcally calibrated and registered a Trosen Robotics PincherX100 robot arm using an RGBD camera. The arm was then tasked to locate and grasp a pen and pass it along to other robot arms (video courtesy of Zhengxiao Han).

  • Mobile Manipulation - Northwestern MS Robotics

    Mobile Manipulation is the final project for ME449 Robot Manipulation.

    For this project, I integrated the concepts of robotic manipulation, forward kinematics, mobile odometry and linear control, and created a simulation of a kuka youBot picking a box up and placing it down in another location with CoppeliaSim.
